About me

My name is Linnea and I'm a photographer!
I started taking photos in 2012 on my first digital camera. After that my love for photography grew and in 2014 I got my first DSLR camera. Since then I've taken thousands of photos, mostly of nature, landscapes and animals. I love to capture beautiful moments and love to share them with others, and now I get to share them with you!
